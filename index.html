About Me

I am currently a Full Stack Developer Student at Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp, and have been learning HTML, CSS, Git, and Markdown. Aside from learning these languages from the bootcamp, I am taking a full stack developer course through Codecademy and a Python course through Udemy. I decided to make this career change because I wanted to challenge myself and I knew I had to make lifestyle changes. I have a good understanding of software and hardware, and have built my own gaming PC. I graduated from the University of Georgia with a B.S. Ed in Exercise Science with the intention of going to Physical Therapy School. I gained healthcare experience at Emory Healthcare then transitioned as their Patient Access Scheduler. Many years in school and professional work settings have given me the skillset to adapt to almost any situation. Currently, I schedule therapy sessions for over 40 patients at an inpatient rehab hospital, which requires energy and attention to fine detail. I also have multiple year of experience as a NASM Ceritifed Personal Trainer, which required strategic planning and customer service for each client.
